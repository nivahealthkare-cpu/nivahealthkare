Frequently Asked Questions

What types of diagnostic scans do you offer?

We offer a range of diagnostic scans including MRI Scan, CT Scan, PET Scan, Ultrasound, X-Ray, and DEXA Scan through certified diagnostic centers.

What is an MRI scan used for?

An MRI scan provides detailed images of organs, soft tissues, joints, brain, and spine, helping doctors diagnose various medical conditions.

When is a CT scan recommended?

A CT scan is commonly recommended for detailed imaging of bones, organs, internal injuries, and certain medical conditions where quick diagnosis is required.

What is the purpose of a PET scan?

A PET scan helps evaluate organ and tissue function and is often used in cancer evaluation, neurological conditions, and cardiac assessments.

What does an Ultrasound scan check?

Ultrasound scans are used to examine internal organs, pregnancy-related assessments, abdominal conditions, and soft tissue structures using sound waves.

How long does it take to receive scan reports?

Report availability depends on the scan type. Most scan reports are typically shared within a few hours to 24 hours.

Is preparation required before a scan?

Some scans may require preparation such as fasting or removing metal objects. Specific instructions will be shared at the time of booking.

How do I choose the right scan for my condition?

The appropriate scan is usually advised by a doctor based on your symptoms and medical history. Our team can help with booking once the scan is prescribed.
