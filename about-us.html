Welcome to Niva Health Kare

Where care meets precision — your trusted destination for diagnostics, consultations, and advanced medical care.

Founded in 2025, Niva Health Kare has evolved from a focused diagnostic centre into a multidisciplinary healthcare hub serving communities across Bangalore and beyond. With expert consultants, modern facilities, and patient-first service, we redefine accessible and reliable healthcare.

Our specialties range from preventive health checkups to critical surgical procedures — supported by world-class equipment, skilled professionals, and an environment of empathy and trust. At Niva Health Kare, every patient matters, every result counts.

Our Vision

To redefine healthcare by integrating advanced diagnostics, expert consultations, and surgical care — all under one trusted name. Our vision is to make modern, ethical, and accessible healthcare available to every individual and family.

At NIVA Health kare, we aim to build a healthier community through innovation, precision, and compassion. From early diagnosis to successful recovery, we ensure every patient receives the right care at the right time.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our dedicated team of specialized doctors, surgeons, diagnostic experts, and healthcare professionals who work together to deliver holistic, patient-focused treatment and care.

Guided by expertise, compassion, and innovation, our multidisciplinary team ensures seamless coordination across diagnostics, surgeries, and wellness programs. Together, we uphold NIVA Health kare’s promise — “Expert Care, Trusted Results.”

Founder & Managing Director:
Dr. Javaji Ravi Prasad
MD, DMRD – Professor of Radiology

A distinguished medical professional with over 35 years of expertise in Radiology and Medical Education, Dr. Javaji Ravi Prasad is the visionary Founder and Managing Director of NIVA Health kare. As a Professor at Raja Rajeshwari Medical College, he has guided numerous postgraduate students, inspiring them toward excellence in diagnostic and clinical practice. Driven by a passion for precision, technology, and compassionate service, Dr. Javaji Ravi Prasad established NIVA Health Kare in 2025 at Chandra Layout, Bengaluru. He is also the founder of Vinayaka Hospital and Raghavendra Diagnostic Center, which have been successfully serving patients across Bengaluru for over 25 years, providing high-quality and trusted healthcare services.
