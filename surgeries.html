    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. Who will collect my sample? +
    A certified phlebotomist from NivaHealthKare will visit your home at the scheduled time to collect samples safely and professionally.
    2. Can I reschedule my appointment? +
    Yes, appointments can be rescheduled up to 2 hours before your selected slot through your booking details on our website.
    3. Do I need to fast before certain tests? +
    Some tests, such as Fasting Blood Sugar and Lipid Profile, require fasting. Specific instructions are shared during booking and via reminders.
    4. How soon will I receive my reports? +
    Most reports are delivered within 6 to 24 hours via SMS, email, and WhatsApp for your convenience.
    5. Are your labs certified? +
    Yes. NivaHealthKare partners only with NABL-certified and ISO-standard labs to ensure accuracy, safety, and reliability.
    6. Is home sample collection safe? +
    Yes. Our phlebotomists follow strict hygiene protocols. All equipment is sterilized and replaced after every use for your safety.
    7. Can I consult a doctor after receiving my reports? +
    Yes. NivaHealthKare provides affordable or complimentary doctor consultations for most tests after your reports are available.
    8. What if I miss my appointment? +
    If you miss your slot, you can reschedule at no extra cost. Kindly inform us in advance to avoid repeat-visit charges.
    9. How can I pay for my tests? +
    NivaHealthKare supports secure payment options including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash during sample collection.
    10. Is my personal data safe? +
    Yes. Your personal and health data is encrypted, securely stored, and handled in compliance with healthcare data protection standards.
