You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information held by Niva Health Kare. You can contact our support team to exercise these rights.

We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, ensuring your privacy is respected at all times.

Our website and services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.
